About

Local Madison Barber Eddie Luna has worked hard to maintain and establish his very own business, Neat Fades Barbershop, here in his hometown of Madison, WI. Against the odds of opening during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020.

We offer a full range of grooming services, from meticulous beard trims and traditional straight razor shaves, to personalized styling tailored to your hair type and preferences.

Owner - Eddie Luna

Amenities

Appointments

Walk-in appointments are welcome! However we encourage you to schedule one in advance! If you need to cancel your appointment, please do so two hours in advance in order to avoid being put on the list of no-show or no-call.

Services

Wi-Fi, TV, Vending Machine, Quality Seats, Mirrors, ATM machine, lighting and additional equipment.
