About

Hello! I'm Raul, an Entry-level Web Developer based in Madison, Wisconsin. This is my portfolio, the main link to my projects, Web-Dev experiences and capabilities. I'm a hungry learner, always wanting to learn and take in the ideas of others, then adapting them to create beautiful websites.

There's something to me very facinating and expressive about UI/UX design and being able to create something from your own personal experience.

I'm also entirely self taught! Just pure dedication and determination!
